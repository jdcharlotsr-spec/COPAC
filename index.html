OUR MISSION

OUR VISION

A city transformed by the power of the Holy Ghost, where every individual discovers their divine purpose and walks in their God-given destiny.

WHAT IS APOSTOLIC?

To be Apostolic means we are built upon the foundation of the early church described in the Book of Acts. We embrace the life-changing power of the Holy Spirit, the beauty of authentic worship, and the truth of the Word of God. At The IAM Church, our Apostolic identity fuels our distinctive call to help you discover your divine purpose, pursue your God-given passion, and raise the praise in your everyday life.
